First post!

2019-10-19

Hi and welcome. This is blog built to showcase my projects and experiments in data, statistics, and programming, with some discourse about product management thrown in. Since all of my work is proprietary, one of my goals here is to show off my skills using Driven Data competitions or similar projects. Opinions here are my own and do not reflect those of my employer.

This site is built using blogdown in R, using the Hugo Lithium theme @jrutheiser/hugo-lithium-theme and modified by Yihui Xie. A huge thanks to everyone in the open source community who built the myriad to tools that bring you this webpage.
