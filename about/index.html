About

Hello and welcome! I’m a software product manager and data scientist who builds software products from idea to market launch. I manage digital products and analytics at G100 Companies, but any work and opinions here are my own and do not reflect those of my employer.

My background is in environmental science and I started my career in the hydroelectric power industry before working on geospatial mapping products at Elsevier, Inc.. I’m a graduate of the CUNY M.S. Data Science program.
